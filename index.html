Renewables
Renewable energy is a crucial step for a successful energy transition and to make the most of these technologies, their proper use is essential. The efficiency and effectiveness of renewable energy depend not only on the type of power plant, but also on its location.
Instructions

Determine which is the best renewable power plant for the location indicated on the map.

Identify which of these three regions corresponds to the investment map.

Identify which area of the region the point on the map refers to.
Each area corresponds to one of the following forms of renewable energy.
Refer to the table below and choose the type of power plant corresponding to the correct form of energy.
Fossil Fuels
Our nation, like most countries that consume fossil fuels, must import these resources, whose prices fluctuate significantly due to demand, supply and geopolitical balances that can drastically change costs with little or no warning.
Instructions

Determine which of the following nations is more convenient to establish a fossil fuel import agreement with.

Start by understanding what type of resource the investment involves.
Two agreements with two different nations will be available, which you can identify through their flags.
The resource price changes depending on the exporter and its geopolitical condition.

Cross-reference the nation with its geopolitical situation to find the resource price per unit.

Select the most advantageous option.
Nuclear Power
Nuclear energy is rapidly developing and has significant potential to meet the nation's energy needs. However, as it grows, the number of practices, regulations, procedures and requirements to comply with for building new plants that meet all standards increases.
Instructions

Ensure that the steps for constructing a new plant are carried out in the correct order.

Start by determining the type of practice using the code provided on the investment.
Now guide the Minister of Energy through the correct order of steps to follow. But be careful, they will only see the abbreviated names.
Public Approval
In every nation, public approval is the foundation of stability. Ensuring that the population understands and supports political decisions is essential for harmony and progress. To achieve this, clear communication and effective education are the cornerstones of a satisfied and well-informed society.
Instructions

It is your responsibility to ensure that the chosen medium is appropriate for the target audience of the information.

Start by analyzing the charts presented to the Energy Minister, identifying the specific target audience. Each chart highlights the key criteria of each archetype, assigning them a score.

Once the target is identified, the focus shifts to communication channels. Present the possible media options to the Minister of Energy, who must ensure that only the correct responses are approved before confirming the investment and launching a new awareness campaign.
Strategic Assets
The energy transition requires the implementation of new technologies, the updating of infrastructure, transportation and the electrical grid. When it comes to innovation, everyone wants to voice their opinion, but it is crucial, especially when the stakes are high, to listen to those who have in-depth knowledge of the topic.
Instructions

The Minister of Energy will meet with various experts, but must pay attention only to the expert specialized in the topic at hand.

Identify the current theme of the investment through its title and link each expert to their opinion starting from their profiles.

Make sure to listen only to the relevant opinions.
